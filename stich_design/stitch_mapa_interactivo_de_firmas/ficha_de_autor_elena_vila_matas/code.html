Elena Vila-Matas

"The Rose in the Labyrinth"

Exploring the ethereal boundaries between Barcelona's gothic history and the contemporary pulse of the city, Vila-Matas has become the definitive voice of the new Catalan literary renaissance. Her prose, both sharp and melodic, captures the ephemeral beauty of Sant Jordi like no other.
